About Us


Someone once said, "Tired of London Tired of Life"

London covers over 600 square miles and is home to more then seven million people. Our city is one of the most creative and diverse in the world, containing art, literature and history of mankind. However, it is fair to say that more often than not we forget how lucky we are to be here.

We at London Walking Tours, have set ourselves a challenge to help you discover London's hidden gems, from sight-seeing to food markets to picturesque tours you are sure to enjoy it and look at London with new eyes.
